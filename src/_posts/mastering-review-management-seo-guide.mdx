### The 3 Pillars of Review SEO

1. **Freshness Matters:** A 5-star review from 2021 carries far less weight than a 4-star review from last week. Automated tools help you encourage a steady stream of new feedback.
2. **Keywords in User Content:** You can't control what users write, but you *can* influence it. Asking specific questions ("How was the pizza crust?") encourages keyword-rich answers ("The deep dish crust was amazing!").
3. **Engagement is Ranking:** High responses rates correlate strongly with higher map pack rankings. Aim for 100% response rate, even for negative reviews.

## Turning Reviews into Content & Backlinks

Reviews are user-generated content (UGC). UGC is a goldmine for SEO if leveraged correctly outside of just Google Maps.

### 1. The "Wall of Love" Strategy

Create a dedicated /reviews page on your site. Schema mark it properly. This page will often rank for "Your Brand Reviews" searches, protecting your brand reputation.

### 2. Social Proof as Content

Take screenshots of your best reviews and turn them into Instagram Stories or LinkedIn posts. 
Tag the user (if appropriate) to build community. 

### 3. Getting Backlinks

If you receive a review from a local influencer or another business owner, reach out to them! 
Thank them for the review and ask if they'd be open to a partnership or a featured blog post. 
This can lead to high-quality local backlinks.
