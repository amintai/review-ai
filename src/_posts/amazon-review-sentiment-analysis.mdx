## The Star Rating Mirage

Star ratings are a "coarse" metric. They don't differentiate between someone who loved the color but hated the performance, and someone who hated everything.

### Why Stars Can Be Misleading:
- **Rating Inflation**: Sellers often use "review seeding" to get an initial burst of 5-star ratings.
- **Old Success, New Failure**: A product might have 4.5 stars from three years ago, but the latest version (the one you'll receive) is actually lower quality.
- **The "Good for the Price" Bias**: Humans often rate things 5 stars simply because they were cheap, not because they were actually good.

## What is Sentiment Analysis?

Sentiment analysis is an AI process that goes beyond the "number" and analyzes the **emotional tone** and **specificity** of the written text.

### How AI Analyzes Sentiment:
1. **Polarity Mapping**: Identifying words like "fantastic," "garbage," "disappointed," or "impressed."
2. **Contextual Weighting**: Understanding that "this phone is a beast!" is positive, while "this phone is a disaster!" is negative.
3. **Feature Attribution**: Connecting sentiment to specific attributes (e.g., "The **screen** is beautiful" vs "The **battery** is terrible").
