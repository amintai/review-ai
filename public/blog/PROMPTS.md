# AI Image Generation Prompts for ReviewAI Blog

Use these prompts with Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion to generate blog images.

## General Style Guidelines
- Modern, clean, professional aesthetic
- ReviewAI brand colors: Orange (#F97316), Blue (#3B82F6), Purple (#8B5CF6)
- Flat design or subtle 3D elements
- Tech-forward, data-driven feel
- No faces (to avoid licensing issues)

## 1. Fake Reviews Detection Image

**Filename:** `fake-reviews-detection.jpg`

**Midjourney Prompt:**
```
A modern flat design illustration showing a magnifying glass examining Amazon-style 5-star reviews with red warning flags and X marks over fake reviews, clean white background, orange and red accent colors, professional tech aesthetic, data visualization elements, --ar 2:1 --style modern --v 6
```

**DALL-E Prompt:**
```
Create a professional blog header image showing fake review detection. Include a magnifying glass examining star ratings, red warning flags, checkmarks for real reviews and X marks for fake ones. Modern flat design style with orange and red accents on white background. 1200x630px, tech-forward aesthetic.
```

## Alternative: Canva Templates

If AI generation isn't available, use Canva:

1. Go to Canva.com
2. Select "Blog Banner" (1200x630px)
3. Search for templates: "tech blog", "data visualization", "shopping"
4. Customize with ReviewAI brand colors
5. Add relevant icons from Canva library
6. Export as JPG (optimized)
