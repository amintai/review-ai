## Image Sources

### Option 1: AI-Generated Images (Recommended)
- Use Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion
- Prompts provided in PROMPTS.md
- Ensure commercial license

### Option 2: Stock Photos
- Unsplash (free, commercial use)
- Pexels (free, commercial use)
- Shutterstock (paid, high quality)

### Option 3: Custom Design
- Hire designer on Fiverr/Upwork
- Budget: $20-50 per image
- Provide brand guidelines (orange/blue colors, modern style)

## Technical Specifications

- **Format:** JPG (optimized for web)
- **Dimensions:** 1200x630px (2:1 ratio for social sharing)
- **File size:** <200KB (optimized)
- **Alt text:** Descriptive for SEO and accessibility
- **Naming:** kebab-case (e.g., fake-reviews-detection.jpg)
